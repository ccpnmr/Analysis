Pick and Assign: Assessing your Data

The Pick & Assign module works beautifully with beautiful data, but if your data is more challenging, you may find you struggle at first. Here are a few ways to investigate your data and make changes to enable to you successfully pick and assign your 3D spectra.

Increasing visible z-planes

Sometimes you may find that the peaks which are picked are in a neighbouring plane to the one you are currently looking at, or indeed in a plane even further away. This makes it difficult to assess quickly whether all the peaks were picked correctly.
