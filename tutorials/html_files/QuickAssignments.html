Quick Assignments

You can make very quick assignments at any point by left-dragging an NmrResidue or NmrAtom from the sidebar onto one or more selected peaks. When you release the NmrResidue or NmrAtom in the spectrum display you will be asked if you want to Create Marks, Assign NmrResidues/NmrAtoms to selected peaks/multiplets, Mark and Assign or Cancel. Select one of the Assign options to make an assignment. If you have made a mistake, simply undo it with Ctrl+Z (Cmd+Zon a Mac) or just drag a different NmrResidue/Atom onto the peak.
